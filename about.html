THE AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF THE UICC

The UICC has been established with the explicit aim of deepening partnerships between the UAE and Indian business communities.

The UICC’s core objectives are to:

  • Encourage inclusive and diverse partnerships across all sectors of the two business communities, with a strong emphasis on critical industries such as startups, women entrepreneurs, micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises (MSMEs), and the services sectors;
  • facilitate a range of events that bring the two business communities together in a manner that supports trust and dialogue; and
  • provide the two business communities with the resources they need to truly succeed, and fully unlock the benefits and opportunities of the CEPA.

Through a robust lineup of engagements, bilateral dialogue and networking opportunities, the UICC is there to support the business needs of both UAE and Indian companies seeking to enter or expand into either market.
